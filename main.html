Invasion

For almost 20 years, we have thrown a party on the last week of October where other chapters, fraternities, and sororities from New Jersey Boston come to Binghamton to party with us. Invasion is the largest party in Binghamton over Halloween weekend, and influencers such as Hena Yang and Nina Lin have attended in the past. While the brothers of Nu Alpha Phi work hard to plan and execute this event, we always make sure to have fun with guests and each other.
